Display WaveShare 7.5-v2
------------------------

To start an application with this backend pass the '-b
display:WaveShare-7.5-v2' on the application commandline.

.Links
link:https://www.waveshare.com/w/upload/6/60/7.5inch_e-Paper_V2_Specification.pdf[Specification]
link:https://www.waveshare.net/datasheet/EK_PDF/EK79655.pdf[GD7965 datasheet]

.Display parameters
[cols=",3",options="header"]
|====================================================================
| Name            | WaveShare-7.5-v2
| Type            | e-ink
| Driver IC       | GD7965 aka UC8179c
| Depth           | 1 BPP
| Resolution      | 800x480
| Size            | 170.2 mm x 111.2 mm
| DPI             | 125
| Full refresh    | \~4s OTP LUT, ~3s GFXprim LUT
| Partial refresh | \~4s OTP LUT, ~1s GFXprim LUT
|====================================================================
